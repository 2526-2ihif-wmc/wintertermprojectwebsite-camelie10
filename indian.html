INDIAN FOOD

★★ Advanced

Biryani

Fragrant rice layered with spiced meat or vegetables, slow-cooked to absorb rich flavors. Known for its aroma and saffron color. A staple in Indian celebrations.

★ Easy

Butter Chicken

Creamy tomato-based curry with tender chicken pieces. Mild, rich, and slightly sweet, popular worldwide. Great for beginners.

★ Easy

Garlic Naan

Soft flatbread baked in a tandoor, topped with garlic and butter. Perfect for scooping up curries and currying favor at the dinner table.

★★★ Hard

Tandoori Chicken

Chicken marinated in yogurt and spices, roasted in a tandoor. Smoky, juicy, and brightly colored. Requires skill and high heat to get the perfect char.

★★ Advanced

Dal Makhani

Slow-cooked black lentils with butter and cream. Rich, comforting, and full of flavor. Patience is key for creamy perfection.

★ Easy

Palak Paneer

Spinach curry with soft paneer cubes. Mild, creamy, and nutritious, often eaten with naan or rice. Quick and healthy choice.

★ Easy

Pakora

Crispy vegetable fritters coated in spiced chickpea flour. Popular street snack, especially during rainy season.

★★ Advanced

Pani Puri

Hollow crispy shells filled with spiced water, potatoes, and chickpeas. Tangy, spicy, and refreshing. Needs practice to assemble perfectly.

★★ Advanced

Chicken Tikka Masala

Grilled chicken pieces in spiced tomato and cream sauce. Bold, rich, and slightly smoky. Popular globally, requires careful grilling.

★ Easy
sweet

Gulab Jamun

Deep-fried milk dough balls soaked in rose-flavored sugar syrup. Sweet and melt-in-your-mouth dessert, simple but indulgent.

ITALIAN

Pizza

★ Easy
Personal favorite

This one is probably the most popular italian dish ever.
Pizza comes from Naples, Italy, and it became popular worldwide because it's simple and tasty. It starts with fresh dough, tomato sauce, and melted mozzarella and then you can add anything from veggies to pepperoni and even seafood.
But of course you can also just leave it as the iconic Margherita.

Spaghetti Bolognese

★ Easy

Another absolute classic from Italian cuisine.
Spaghetti Bolognese comes from the city of Bologna and became a worldwide favorite because it's rich, hearty, and tasty. It's made with spaghetti topped with a slow-cooked meat sauce full of tomatoes, onions, garlic, and herbs.

Tagliatelle

★★ Advanced

Tagliatelle is a wide, ribbon-shaped pasta from the Emilia-Romagna region of Italy. Its shape makes it ideal for holding creamy sauces or traditional meat ragù. This pasta is often handmade and is known for its soft yet slightly firm texture.

Ravioli

★★★ Hard

Ravioli are like little pasta gifts with a delicious surprise inside. Filled with cheese, meat, or vegetables, they bring a lot of flavor in every bite. Paired with the right sauce, ravioli feel both comforting and special.

Gnocchi

★★ Advanced

Gnocchi may look simple, but they are soft, fluffy, and full of comfort. Made mainly from potatoes, they soak up sauces beautifully. Whether served with pesto, tomato sauce, or butter and sage, they're always a great choice.

Risotto

★★ Advanced

Risotto is creamy, rich, and all about patience and flavor. Slowly cooked rice creates a smooth texture that feels almost luxurious. From mushrooms to seafood, every version delivers deep and satisfying taste.

Lasagna

Personal favorite
★★ Advanced

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that's impossible not to love. With layers of pasta, rich sauce, and melted cheese, it's packed with flavor. It's hearty, comforting, and perfect when you're really hungry.

Bruschetta

★ Easy

Bruschetta is a traditional Italian appetizer made with grilled bread. It is usually topped with fresh tomatoes, olive oil, garlic, and basil. This dish is loved for its fresh taste and simple ingredients.

Salad Caprese

★ Easy

Caprese salad is a fresh Italian dish made with mozzarella, tomatoes, and basil. It comes from the island of Capri and highlights high-quality ingredients. Caprese is often served as a light appetizer or side dish.

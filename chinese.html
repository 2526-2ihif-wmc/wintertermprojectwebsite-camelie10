About These Dishes

Fried Rice

Stir-fried rice with vegetables, egg, and optional meat or seafood. A very popular, quick meal eaten across China. It can be customized easily and is a staple for many households.

★ Easy

Chow Mein

Stir-fried noodles with vegetables, meat or tofu, and soy sauce. Crispy or soft depending on preference. It’s a fast, satisfying dish suitable for lunch or dinner.

★ Easy

Sweet and Sour Chicken

Crispy fried chicken coated in a bright, tangy sweet-and-sour sauce made from vinegar, sugar, and sometimes pineapple. A colorful and flavorful dish often enjoyed worldwide.

★★ Advanced

Dongpo Pork

Pork belly slowly braised in soy sauce, Shaoxing wine, and sugar until tender and caramelized. A rich, traditional dish that requires patience and careful cooking.

★★★ Hard

Peking Duck

Crispy-skinned roasted duck served with pancakes, scallions, cucumber, and sweet bean sauce. Requires special technique to achieve the perfect crispy skin and tender meat.

★★ Advanced Personal favorite

Dumplings / Jiaozi

Thin dough filled with vegetables, meat, or seafood, then boiled, steamed, or pan-fried. These are my personal favorite! Eating them is a traditional custom, especially during celebrations.

★★ Advanced Personal favorite

Mapo Tofu

Soft tofu in a spicy chili and peppercorn sauce with optional minced meat. Known for its bold flavor and numbing spiciness, this dish is a staple of Sichuan cuisine.

★★ Advanced

Spring Rolls

Thin rolls filled with vegetables or meat, then deep-fried until golden and crispy. Traditionally served during festivals and gatherings as an appetizer or snack.

★★ Advanced

Tomato and Egg Stir-Fry

A simple, comforting home-style dish made by stir-frying tomatoes and eggs. Quick to cook and a favorite for casual meals in many families.

★ Easy

Wonton Soup

Light broth with dumplings filled with pork, shrimp, or vegetables. Delicate and warming, this dish is enjoyed as a starter or main course across China.

★★ Advanced

Xiao Long Bao

Soup dumplings with a delicate wrapper and hot broth inside. Requires careful folding technique and precise steaming, making it a more challenging dish to prepare at home.

★★★ Hard
